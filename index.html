HOW IT WORKS

STEP 1

Once users make an order on your website they will see an offer to choose a special gift from one of our partners.

STEP 2

By clicking on the button users are redirected to a personalized page with the most relevant gifts from non-rival brands.

STEP 3

Once users select the offer, they will be redirected to a corresponding website and will see a coupon code in the top bar.

STEP 4

Similarly, those users who completed an order on other websites will be able to see your offer as well.
